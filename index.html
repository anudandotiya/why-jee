Is Jee easy??

This is the question which comes in every students opting for jee mains and adv. In reality the mains exam is much simpler than advance but the competition makes the mains exam too difficult for the student. If we see with a rough data approximately 10 lac students gives jee mains and 1.25 lac student gives jee advance (the number may vary from the original numbers)

Now with this number (10 lac) students which appear in exam approx 1.25 lac students gets selected in jee advanced which means mathematically you 1 out of 10 chances of just to appear in jee advanced.

Actually in reality you just have competition with those students who got elected in jee advanced paper rest of the aspirants are not committed for the exam they just want to feel exam atmosphere also who just have taken math for school academics or for any other competition although these type of students are present in every competition exams but they are numerous in JEE Ratio remains the same for advanced also and from 1 lac students almost 10,000 are those which gets IIT and approximately top 7,000 students gets good IIT with any compromised branch or vice-versa approximately top 3,000 students gets good IIT with a good branch.

Is there any need of private coaching??

There are varieties of coaching center which provides coaching from 6th-7th class which makes their foundation strong and through that they can decide when the time comes to take decision of the subject for future. Most of the JEE aspirants take coaching from 9th to 12th class and fights in the competition .It is the Govt. which has not left any option for the students who wants a good collage since there are no weightage of 12th marks also there are no other methods for entering the collages without fighting this competition so for a good rank in my opinion you have to take private coaching atleast 11th onwards.

How you should start your preparation-

This question often occurs in every parents and students that when should they should start their preparation and how they should approach the exam. Now its all upto your interest and ability for starting your preparation . I think from 9th is the best time to start your foundation and have a complete exam preparation in 11th and 12th.

The question paper is divided into 3 parts subjectwise Physics , Chemistry , Maths

Its my personal observation that the common thing which is required in every subject is the calculation even in chemistry. If you have a good hand in calculation I am assuring you that you will be accurate and this fast calculation will save your time.
